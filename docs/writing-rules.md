---

## 🎙️ 1. Narration

The voice of the saga is inviolable:

- 🏰 The narrator always speaks with the tone of a **traditional epic fantasy novel**
- 😐 The narrator **never** jokes, winks at the audience, or acknowledges how ridiculous the events are
- 🕯️ Every event is described with **complete sincerity**

---

## 🎭 2. Humor

> **The humor comes from contrast.**

| Do | Don't |
|:---|:---|
| ✅ Present ridiculous situations as completely normal within the world | ❌ Have characters question the absurdity around them (they rarely do) |
| ✅ Let the seriousness of the prose carry the comedy | ❌ Use modern internet humor, memes, or references that may become dated |

The joke must still land in a hundred years. Write accordingly.

---

## 🧝 3. Characters

Every soul in the kingdom obeys four laws:

1. 🎯 **Consistency** — characters behave consistently throughout the series
2. 🗣️ **Distinction** — every major character has a distinct personality, motivation, and speaking style
3. 🧠 **Memory** — characters remember previous adventures and reference them naturally
4. 🌱 **Growth** — recurring characters grow and develop over time

---

## 🌍 4. World Building

The five pillars of the persistent world:

```
   🏰 Locations evolve.
   📚 History accumulates.
   🏺 Artifacts return.
   ⚡ Events have lasting consequences.
   🚫 Nothing is reset between chapters.
```

The world is persistent. Always.

---

## 🔗 5. Continuity

- 📜 Everything that happens becomes canon unless intentionally revised
- 🧱 Future stories build upon previous adventures
- 🔁 Callbacks are encouraged — **when they serve the story**
- ⚖️ **Consistency is more important than introducing new ideas**

---

## 📖 6. Story Structure

Every chapter follows the established story formula.

```
   FIXED FORMULA  +  RANDOM MAD LIB  =  UNIQUE ADVENTURE
   (consistency)     (chaos)            (canon)
```

Readers should be able to enjoy each chapter independently — while those who follow the full saga are rewarded.

---

## 🏛️ 7. Canon Rules

| Rule | Decree |
|:---:|:---|
| 🔒 **Finalization** | Once a chapter is finalized, its events become official history |
| 🌐 **Induction** | Major characters, locations, creatures, and artifacts become part of the shared universe |
| ✍️ **Revision** | Canon changes only through intentional revisions or future editions — never by accident |

---

## 💬 8. Dialogue

- 🗣️ Every character has a **unique voice**
- 🎭 Dialogue feels natural and supports each character's personality
- 😂 Humor comes from the situation and the characters — never forced jokes
- 📢 Avoid excessive exposition through dialogue (the Historian handles the lore)

---

## 🎨 9. Creative Freedom

Mad Lib responses are intentionally unpredictable. **That is the point.**

The challenge — and the craft — is to incorporate *every* response into a coherent fantasy story.

Creative problem solving is encouraged, always within the bounds of established canon. When the dice hand you an enchanted toaster, the question is never *whether* it fits the world. The question is *where in the world it has been hiding all this time.*

---

## ⚔️ 10. The Non-Negotiable Rules

<div align="center">

*Carved above the door of the Scriptorium:*

</div>

> 1. ✨ **Every absurd idea is treated with complete seriousness.**
> 2. 🧓 **Grandpa Bibblebop is a reluctant hero.**
> 3. 🎙️ **The narrator never breaks character.**
> 4. 🏰 **The world always takes itself seriously.**
> 5. 🌱 **Every chapter expands the universe.**
> 6. 🔗 **Continuity matters.**
> 7. 📜 **Canon is respected.**
> 8. ⏳ **The humor should remain timeless.**
> 9. 🗺️ **Readers should feel like they are exploring a living fantasy world.**
