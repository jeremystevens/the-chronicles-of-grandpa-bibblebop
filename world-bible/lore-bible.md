## 📋 Overview

This document establishes the **foundational lore** of *The Chronicles of Grandpa Bibblebop*.

It defines the general rules that govern the world:

✨ Magic · 🏺 Artifacts · 🐲 Creatures · 📜 Prophecies · 😈 Legendary villains · 🕰️ Historical events

> 📚 Specific entries will be documented separately in their own reference files. This is the constitution; the encyclopedias come later.

---

## ✨ Magic

Magic exists throughout the world and is considered a **normal part of everyday life.**

| Path to Magic | |
|:---|:---:|
| Studied academically | 🎓 |
| Inherited abilities | 🧬 |
| Discovered enchanted objects mid-adventure | 🗝️ |

> ⚖️ **The Law of Magic:** It should feel mysterious and powerful — but it must **never solve every problem.** If magic could fix everything, Grandpa could finally retire. It cannot. He will not.

---

## 🏺 Legendary Artifacts

Legendary artifacts are rare objects with unique histories and abilities.

Every artifact must possess three things:

```
   1. 🌋 AN ORIGIN    — where it came from
   2. 🎯 A PURPOSE    — what it was made to do
   3. 📖 A STORY      — how it ties into the world's history
```

Artifacts may return in future adventures and often become important pieces of the world's lore.

*(The Writing Rules are clear on this: artifacts return. Always. Unfortunately.)*

---

## 🐲 Creatures

The world is home to a wide variety of creatures, from ordinary wildlife to legendary beings.

| Disposition | Role in the World |
|:---:|:---|
| 🕊️ **Friendly** | Allies, companions, and unexpected helpers |
| ⚔️ **Dangerous** | Genuine threats to life, limb, and lunch |
| 🎪 **Neither** | Simply create unusual situations |

> 🌟 Creatures should add **personality and wonder** to the world — never exist only as enemies.

---

## 😈 Villains

Villains come in many forms:

- 👑 Some seek **power**
- 🗡️ Some seek **revenge**
- 🎲 Some simply create **chaos**

The two laws of villainy:

> 1. Each major villain must have **understandable motivations** and leave a lasting impact on the world.
> 2. Defeating a villain must **change the world in some meaningful way.**

*(The Retired Dragon serves as the kingdom's standing proof that even villainy is not permanent — though canon is.)*

---

## 📜 Prophecies

Ancient prophecies are a recurring part of the series.

- 🌅 Prophecies often **begin new adventures**
- 🙇 They are taken **very seriously** by the people of the kingdom
- 🌀 Their wording may be mysterious, confusing, or humorous — but they **always influence the story**

> The kingdom has never once ignored a prophecy. The kingdom has, on many occasions, wished it could.

---

## 🕰️ History

**History is important.**

```
   Adventure happens  ──▶  Historian records it  ──▶  It becomes official history
                                                            │
                     Characters reference it  ◀──  It influences future stories
```

- Every adventure becomes part of the world's official history
- Major events continue to influence future stories
- Characters remember previous adventures and reference them naturally

---

## 🔒 Canon

| Rule | Decree |
|:---:|:---|
| 📖 **Completion** | Once a chapter is completed and approved, its events become official canon |
| ⚖️ **Consistency** | Canon remains consistent throughout the series |
| ✍️ **Revision** | Changes to established lore occur only through intentional revisions |

---

## 🌱 Expanding the Lore

The world is **designed to grow over time.**

New artifacts, creatures, locations, villains, prophecies, and historical events should feel like **natural additions** to the existing world.

> 🧱 Every addition should **strengthen** the overall universe — never replace what already exists.
