## 📋 Overview

This document records the **official chronology** of major historical events within *The Chronicles of Grandpa Bibblebop*.

Only **completed and approved adventures** are added to this timeline. It serves as the definitive historical record of the series and will continue to grow as new stories become canon.

```
        THE AGE OF LEGENDS ────▶ THE RETIREMENT ────▶ THE INTERRUPTIONS
        (Grandpa's career)       (one quiet day)      (everything since)
```

# 🌅 Before the Adventures

## 🏡 Grandpa Bibblebop Retires

After a lifetime of legendary adventures, Grandpa Bibblebop retires to a quiet cottage on the outskirts of Bumblebutt Junction.

His goal is simple:

> ### *Enjoy a peaceful retirement.*

**History has other plans.**

# 📜 The Chronicle of Adventures 📜

</div>

## 🐉 Chapter I — The Dragon of Bumblebutt Junction

> **Status:** ✅ Canon

Grandpa Bibblebop is forced out of retirement to confront the Dragon of Bumblebutt Junction.

The adventure introduces the kingdom to a **new era of legendary events** and establishes Grandpa as the reluctant hero of the modern age.

---

## 🛁 Chapter II — The Public Bath House Incident

> **Status:** ✅ Canon

A mysterious series of events centered around the kingdom's Public Bath House once again interrupts Grandpa's peaceful retirement.

The incident becomes one of the most unusual — **and frequently debated** — events in the kingdom's recorded history.

*(The historians are still arguing about it. Insignificant details are involved.)*

---

## 🛋️ Chapter III — The Curse of the Chrono-Couch of Eternal Sloth

> **Status:** ⏳ In Progress — Pending Canonization

Grandpa Bibblebop faces another impossible adventure after an ancient prophecy threatens the kingdom.
