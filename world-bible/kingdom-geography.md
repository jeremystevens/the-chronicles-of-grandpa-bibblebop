## 📋 Overview

This document defines the geography of the world in which **The Chronicles of Grandpa Bibblebop** takes place.

The geography serves as the foundation for future adventures and provides consistency for recurring locations.

> 🌱 The world should continue to **expand naturally** as new stories are written.

---

<div align="center">

# 👑 The Kingdom of Bumblebutt Junction 👑

### *Peaceful. Prosperous. Perpetually Imperiled.*

</div>

The Kingdom of Bumblebutt Junction serves as the **primary setting** for the series.

Despite being a peaceful and prosperous kingdom, it has an unfortunate reputation for attracting:

| | |
|:---:|:---|
| 📜 | Ancient prophecies |
| 🌋 | Magical catastrophes |
| 🏺 | Legendary artifacts |
| 💀 | World-ending threats |

The citizens have become **surprisingly accustomed** to these events.

---

## 🏰 The Capital City

The capital serves as the **political and cultural center** of the kingdom.

It is home to:

- 🏰 The Royal Castle
- 🖼️ The Royal Museum
- 📚 The Royal Archives
- 🧺 Public marketplaces
- 🛁 The Public Bath House

> Most major adventures begin somewhere within the capital.

---

## 🏘️ Villages & Settlements

Smaller villages are scattered throughout the kingdom.

- These settlements provide opportunities for future stories
- Each may be expanded individually as needed
- Every village should have its own **unique identity and history**

---

## 🌲 Forests

The kingdom contains several **ancient forests**, many filled with:

🏚️ Forgotten ruins · 🦄 Magical creatures · 🗝️ Long-lost secrets

> 📝 *Survey note:* Not every forest needs to be named immediately. New forests may be introduced naturally through future adventures.

---

## ⛰️ Mountains

Large mountain ranges surround portions of the kingdom.

**🐉 Dragon Mountain is the most famous.**

Other mountains may contain:

| | | | |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 🛕 Ancient temples | 🕳️ Hidden caves | 🏛️ Lost civilizations | 🏺 Legendary artifacts |

---

## 🌊 Rivers & Lakes

Several rivers connect the kingdom's towns and villages.

Major lakes serve as important landmarks and may become settings for future adventures.

> 📝 *Survey note:* Specific names will be added as the world expands.

---

## 🛤️ Roads

Well-traveled roads connect the major settlements, frequented by:

🧺 Merchants · ⚔️ Adventurers · 🎓 Scholars · 🏇 Royal messengers · 🧓 The occasional wandering hero

> Many adventures begin along these roads. Grandpa has learned to distrust them.

## 🌏 Beyond the Kingdom

The world extends beyond Bumblebutt Junction.

Other kingdoms, cultures, and distant lands exist — but they are **intentionally left undefined** during the early phases of development.

```
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │                                             │
   │        ~ HERE THERE BE FUTURE CANON ~       │
   │                                             │
   │   (deliberately uncharted, pending dice)    │
   │                                             │
   └─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

This allows the world to grow naturally through future stories.

# 🧭 Geography Philosophy

</div>

- 🎯 The geography should **support storytelling rather than restrict it**
- 🌱 New locations should feel like natural additions to the world
- 🕸️ As adventures continue, the kingdom grows richer, more detailed, and more interconnected — while maintaining internal consistency
- 🔁 **Every location introduced should have the potential to return in future chapters**
