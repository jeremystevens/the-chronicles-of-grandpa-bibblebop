# Short-Term Goals

## Continue the Story
- Write Episodes VII and beyond.
- Maintain the serialized "To Be Continued..." format.
- Continue illustrating every episode.

## Keep the World Alive
- Revisit recurring locations naturally.
- Reuse villains, magical artifacts, and creatures when appropriate.
- Introduce new townspeople through short interactions.

# Long-Term Goals

## 50+ Episode Collection

Continue writing one short adventure at a time.

Do not artificially lengthen episodes.

Allow the overall book to become long through many adventures.

---

## Illustration Collection

Create one Victorian-style illustration for every episode.

Maintain a consistent visual identity throughout the series.

---

## Series Bible

Eventually create a concise reference guide containing:

- Character profiles
- Recurring locations
- Recurring villains
- Magical artifacts
- Running jokes
- Illustration guide
- Naming conventions
- Episode checklist

---

# Guiding Principle

Never sacrifice the humor for unnecessary complexity.

Keep every episode:

- Short
- Fast
- Absurd
- Memorable
- Dead-serious

One hilarious adventure at a time.
